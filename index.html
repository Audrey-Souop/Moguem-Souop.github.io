Who am I ?

I am currently a PhD. Researcher specializing in hardware acceleration for robotics systems. My work focuses on designing high-performance, energy-efficient FPGA architectures that enables deterministic execution for safety-critical robotic applications. One of my key project is the development of the Sparse Patch Systolic Array (SPSA), a hardware accelerator that rethinks how robots process spatial information. Alongside this project, I have worked on projects in Robot and Human pose estimation, Computer Vision and System-level Integration using ROS2 and simulation tools. Moreover, I have architecture a multi-agent Banking System using Agentic AI frameworks like AutoGen to automate transaction monitoring and financial reporting for enhanced SLA protection and regulatory compliance.
